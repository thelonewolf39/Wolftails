How We Make Wolf Tails

Behind every frame of Wolf Tails is a glitchy, creative mess made with:

  • 🖌️ Procreate – for hand-drawn backgrounds
  • 🎞️ Canva – for character animation and layout
  • 🎧 Soundtrap – to build eerie music loops
  • 💻 GitHub Pages – to host this very site
  • 🎙️ Google Meets - to help us collab

Everything is drawn, designed, and directed by The Lone Wolf.
